Dr. Li Hongyong joins Aerospace Information Technology University

27 Aug 2025

← All news

We are delighted to announce that Dr. Li Hongyong, a former exchange PhD student from Shandong University, has recently been appointed as an academic staff at Aerospace Information Technology University.

As the first member of the EASE research group, Dr. Li has made significant contributions, taking on key responsibilities such as instrument operation and maintenance, field sampling, and data analysis. Dr. Li has published a number of papers in leading journals, including Atmospheric Chemistry and Physics, ACS Environmental Au, and Environmental Science & Technology.

We extend our heartfelt congratulations to Dr. Li Hongyong on this well-deserved achievement and wish him continued success in his academic career.
