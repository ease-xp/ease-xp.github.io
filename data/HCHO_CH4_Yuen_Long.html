HCHO ＆ CH4 - Yuen Long

Sampling period: 12/12/2024 - 13/1/2025

Sampling location: Yuen Long Air Quality Monitoring Station

Lat ＆ Lon: (22°26′43″ N, 114°1′21″ E)

Equipment: PICARRO

From December 12, 2024, to January 13, 2025, a sampling activity was successfully conducted at the Yuen Long Air Quality Monitoring Station to measure the concentrations of Formaldehyde (HCHO) and Methane (CH4) in the atmosphere. Using the high-precision PICARRO instrument, the study effectively collected gas sample data over a one-month period.

This sampling activity provided crucial data for understanding the air quality in the Yuen Long area, particularly regarding the levels of Formaldehyde and Methane. These data are significant for analyzing the environmental conditions of the region and formulating air pollution control measures.
